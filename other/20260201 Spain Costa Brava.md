### Calella de Palafrugell

- [ ] Main beaches (3 beaches: Llafranc, Calella, Tamariu)
- [ ] Historic fishing village center (whitewashed houses, cobbled streets)
- [ ] Camino de Ronda coastal path
- [ ] Cap Roig botanical gardens
- [ ] Year-round vs seasonal atmosphere assessment
- [ ] Local amenities in town vs nearby Palafrugell (3.5km inland)
- **Distance to Barcelona Airport**: 137 km / 1h 35min - 1h 45min drive
- **Distance to International School Costa Brava (Platja d'Aro)**: ~20-25 min drive
- **Distance to Girona schools**: ~30-35 min drive
- **Housing Market**:
  - Average: €1.2M - €1.5M range
  - Entry point: €795,000+ for houses with gardens/pools
  - Premium: €3.2M+ for villas with panoramic sea views
  - Flats from: €359,500+
  - Note: Regularly exceeds €1M due to limited coastal land, strict urban planning, international demand
- **Year-Round Living**: More businesses open in winter vs other coastal towns. Palafrugell (3.5km) is working town with services year-round
- **Key Features**:
  - One of most picturesque Costa Brava villages
  - Traditional fishing village architecture preserved
  - Beautiful rocky coves (calas)
  - Cap Roig Festival venue (summer cultural events)
  - Traditional Habaneras concerts (first Saturday in July)
  - Camino de Ronda coastal hiking trail
- **Landscape**: Dramatic rocky coastline, pine forests, whitewashed fishermen's houses, authentic character
