Encinitas

San Diego County, California

A laid-back surf town with six distinct communities stretching along the coast north of San Diego. Known for its world-class surf breaks, the San Diego Botanic Garden, excellent schools in the San Dieguito Union district, and a thriving local food scene along the 101. A sweet spot between small-town charm and big-city access.

Del Mar

San Diego County, California

An affluent coastal village known for its iconic racetrack ("Where the Turf Meets the Surf"), blufftop views, and pristine beaches. One of San Diego's most desirable addresses with top-rated schools, upscale dining, and a tight-knit community feel. Del Mar offers a quieter, more exclusive alternative to its neighbors.

San Luis Obispo

Central Coast, California

Consistently ranked among the happiest cities in America, SLO offers a walkable downtown, Cal Poly university energy, stunning Central Coast scenery, and a slower pace of life. Wine country, hiking, and beaches like Avila and Pismo are all within minutes. A genuine small-city feel with a strong sense of community.
